Strawberry Shortcake

Make summertime strawberry shortcake completely from scratch with this recipe!

The summertime dessert that we just can't get out of our heads and tastes delicious any time of the day! Completely homemade strawberry shortcake is easier than you think.

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all purupose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 tsps aluminium free baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tbsps heavy cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Mix the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl. Whisk until combined.
  3. Add the butter and cut into the dry ingredients with a pastry cutter. Pulse until coarse crumbs form.
  4. Pour buttermilk on top and fold everything together with a large spoon until it begins to come together.
  5. Pour the dough onto a floured work surface and using floured hands and a rolling pin flatten into 3/4 inch rectangle.
  6. Cut into 3-inch circles with a biscuit cutter.
  7. Arrange in a 10-inch cast iron skillet.
  8. Brush the tops with heavy cream and coarse sugar.
  9. Bake for 18-22 minutes until biscuits are golden brown.
  10. Slice the biscuits in half and add strawberries and whipped cream on top.
