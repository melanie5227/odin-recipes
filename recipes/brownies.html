Brownies

Best homemade brownies

These brownies will bring back childhood memories of the super fudgy, moist, and chocolaty with crispy edges brownies. They will disappear in no time at all!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3/4 tsp sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup canola oil or extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, water, and vanilla.
  4. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the pan. Bake for 40-48 minutes or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached. Cool completely before slicing.
